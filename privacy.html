Hatch a Legend — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Hatch a Legend ("the app") is a virtual pet game developed and published by Giubera ("we"). This policy describes how the app handles information.

The short version

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

The app does not collect any personal information. It does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not transmit gameplay data to us or to third parties.

Local storage

Your game progress (your pet, its stats, items, and settings) is saved only in the app's local storage on your device. It never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes this data.

Notifications

With your permission, the app schedules local notifications on your device (for example, when your pet finishes a mission or gets hungry). These are generated entirely on your device: no notification data is sent to any server.

Internet, ads and analytics

The app works fully offline. It contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking technologies.

Purchases

The current version of the app contains no in-app purchases. If purchases are added in a future version, they will be processed by Google Play; we will not receive or store your payment details, and this policy will be updated.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience aged 13 and up. Since the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
